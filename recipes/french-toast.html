French Toast

What is French Toast?

French toast is a dish of sliced bread soaked in beaten eggs and often milk or cream, then pan-fried.

The British call french toast 'eggy bread,' 'gypsy bread' or 'french-fried bread.'

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 80ml whole milk
  • 40ml double cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • cinammon (optional)
  • 4 thick slices brioche

Steps

  1. Whisk together eggs, milk, cream and vanilla extract.
  2. Lay the brioche slices in a single layer in a shallow dish and pour the egg mixture over them. Allow to soak for 3-4 minutes.
  3. Lift two slices of brioche out of the dish and add to a frying pan (pre-heated; medium heat). Fry for 3 minutes on each side and remove from pan
  4. Repeat the above steps for remaining slices

Air-Fryer Salmon

Why cook salmon in the air fryer?

Salmon fillets cook beautifully in an air fryer and make a delicious meal when coated in a seasoning and served with greens and grains

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • olive oil
  • salmon fillets

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer. Follow device instructions for preheating.
  2. Combine the salt, pepper, paprika and garlic powder in a bowl. Rub each salmon fillet with olive oil and coat with seasoning.
  3. Place fillets in air fryer basket and cook for 8-10 minutes.
